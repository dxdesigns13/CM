Why she mentors

“Every dish is a love letter to someone.”

I came through Cultured Mornings in 2019, after years of trying to make a kitchen feel like home and failing. What I learned here wasn't just technique — it was that the people who pour into you become the people you pour back into.

I mentor because I remember exactly what it felt like to need someone to believe in me before I could believe in myself.

What mentorship with Lisa looks like
01 / FORMAT
90-minute kitchen sessions
In person at the CM kitchen, hands-on, side-by-side.
02 / CADENCE
Twice a month, for 3 months
Six sessions to start, then both sides decide.
03 / WHERE
Always on CM premises
Program hours, staff present — never anywhere else.
04 / MESSAGING
CM platform only
No personal numbers or DMs; coordinators can see every conversation.
