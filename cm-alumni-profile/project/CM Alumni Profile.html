Why she mentors

"Every dish is a love letter to someone."

I came through Cultured Mornings in 2019, after years of trying to make a kitchen feel like home and failing. What I learned here wasn't just technique — it was that the people who pour into you become the people you pour back into.

I mentor because I remember exactly what it felt like to need someone to believe in me before I could believe in myself. If you're a student wondering whether you have what it takes — you do. I'm not here to tell you that. I'm here to help you figure out for yourself what kind of cook, baker, or human you want to become.

What mentorship with Lisa looks like

A clear, structured rhythm — never a guessing game.

01 / FORMAT
90-minute kitchen sessions
In person, at the Cultured Mornings kitchen. Hands-on, side-by-side. Every session has a purpose you set together at the top.
02 / CADENCE
Twice a month, for 3 months
Six sessions to start. After that you both decide if it's working and want to continue. No pressure either way.
03 / WHERE
Always on CM premises
Sessions happen at the kitchen during program hours. Staff are present. You're never asked to meet anywhere else.
04 / MESSAGING
Through the CM platform only
No personal phone numbers, social DMs, or off-platform contact. Your CM coordinator can see every conversation.
