Jordan's story

Jordan joined Cultured Mornings in fall 2022 — not knowing what to expect. What started as curiosity about food became a calling. In just two years, Jordan grew from a student who had never held a chef's knife to a certified food safety professional who has led catering events and mentored younger participants.

Today, Jordan is working toward a career in hospitality and community catering — with a goal of one day opening a community kitchen right here in Louisville.
