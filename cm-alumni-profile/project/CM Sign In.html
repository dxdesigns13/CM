Mentor · Step 2 of 4

The Mentor Code of Care.

The short version — the full document is in your invite email.

Sessions happen at the Cultured Mornings kitchen with staff present. No contact with mentees outside program settings — no personal numbers, socials, or rides. Guardians are informed and involved. Concerns go to a coordinator, and either side can pause a mentorship at any time, no explanation needed.
